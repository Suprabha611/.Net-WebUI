About Us Our Vision Services
ETG is an edtech company. ETG work towards helping students embrace the industry requirements by exposing them to real world scenarios and providing them with the impetus to apply their skills and knowledge to tackle the issues presented, hence Mr. Himanshu Arora founded this organization that responds to the need of industry ready engineers in the mechanical/automobile segment and to make engineers learn real engineering by working on practical problems and projects. The vision of this organisation is to revolutionize the education scenario across the world by inculcating a pragmatic approach among students towards the academic knowledge imparted by institutions.

  • Industry interaction
